## About me

<img align="right" src="https://media.giphy.com/media/L1R1tvI9svkIWwpVYr/giphy.gif" width="240"/>

I build AI that doctors can trust.

My PhD research sits at the intersection of **Vision Transformers**,
**Explainable AI**, and **Federated Learning** — applied to retinal
disease classification across multi-label fundus datasets. I care
deeply about making AI systems that are not just accurate, but
transparent, privacy-preserving, and clinically deployable.

<br/>

Currently benchmarking **11 ViT architectures** across MuReD,
ODIR-5K, and OIA-ODIR — with best AUC of **0.9826** (DeiT-III).

<br/>

Presented at international conferences. Two publications in progress.

## Featured projects

### Retinal disease classification · ViT benchmarking

> *Benchmarking 11 Vision Transformer architectures on multi-label
> retinal fundus datasets with asymmetric focal loss, sqrt-damped
> class weighting, and post-hoc temperature calibration.*

| Highlight | Detail |
|---|---|
| Best performer (scale) | DeiT-III — AUC 0.9826 on ODIR-5K |
| Best performer (low data) | Swin-B — AUC 0.9402 on MuReD |
| Most scalable | ViT-Large — rank 11 → rank 2 across datasets |
| Ablation gain | +3.9% mAP from loss + calibration pipeline |
